🧠 Cognitive & Thinking Skills

Pattern Recognition

Make a list of every repetitive and manual task in your week and days.

Systems Thinking

How can you combine multi-layered step-by-step processes into one single project space?

First Principle Thinking

Go back to the absolute basics and build it from scratch with AI.

Vision Clarity

If you can't describe what you want, then you don't know what you want.

Critical Evaluation

Read, check, iterate. Don't just max process.

🗨 Communication Skills

Articulation

This is why I use Wispr — for precision, depth, and as much articulation as possible to format the output I want.

Contextual Framing

Making sure AI has enough information and knowledge base so it gives you the right type of output.

Iterative Dialogue

Treat it like a conversation and not a transaction. This is probably the most important skill.

🧡 Self-Awareness Skills

Knowing Your Genius Zones

You can't check something's actually right if you don't actually fucking know it yourself.

Be Honest With Your Bottlenecks

Ego gets in the way of delegation. You think it can't do it as well as you. It can.

Emotional Regulation

Frustration with AI will kill momentum. If you ain't got patience, don't even fucking attempt it.

👑 Leadership & Decision-Making

Prioritisation

You can do a hundred things at once, but don't. Focus on executing one high-level thing at a time.

Judgement

AI gives you unlimited options, but it's still you who has to make the call.

Taste

Read everything on the output so you can formulate the next iterations.

Ownership

Treat your AI outputs as actual responsibility, not just to be lazy.

🎨
Creative Skills

Imagination

Willingness to Experiment
